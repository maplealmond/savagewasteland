The 10mm SMG extended mags is a weapon mod sold in Fallout: New Vegas.

Contents

[show]

    EffectsEdit

    This mod increases the ammunition capacity of the 10mm SMG by ten rounds, from 30 to 40.

    LocationsEdit

    Sold by:

    • Alexander, 188 Trading Post
    • Arms merchant, 188 Trading Post
    • Blake, Crimson Caravan Company
    • Dale Barton, The Fort
    • Vendortron, Gun Runners kiosk

    NotesEdit

    Prior to patch 1.3.0.452, the 10mm SMG mags were only worth 250 caps.
